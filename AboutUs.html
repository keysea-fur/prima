ABOUT PRIMA GAMES

Prima Games is an e-learning website wherein students ranging from Kinder to Grade 3 can visit, sign-in and answer the games and worksheets provided by the developers. The website contains the games, curriculum and the worksheets, an option is supplied for the user to choose whether the student is in Kinder, Grade 1, Grade 2 or Grade 3.

The subject “Mathematics” has been a part of any education system, it is considered as one of the major subjects and it is important to learn more of the topics it have. The math subject is deeply required to be taught in the very beginning of a child’s school experience, from kindergarten to senior year. Prima Games welcomes students from kindergarten up to grade 3 to visit and at the same time learn from our website.

The goal of Prima Games is to enhance the students capability while studying math and to engage them in studying Math through games and worksheets. Prima Games can be considered as a medium for Mathematics education for kids. The developers of Prima Games are unquestionably open about the limited options for the grade levels, whereas seen on the curriculum page of the Prima Games website.
